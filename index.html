Our Philosophy

A coastal sanctuary
above the sea

Perched on limestone cliffs above the Côte d'Azur, Lumara was conceived as a private world where time softens and beauty becomes immersive. Each corner of our estate reflects a deep reverence for the Mediterranean way of life — unhurried, sensory, and deeply nourishing.
